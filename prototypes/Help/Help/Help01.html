OSS Help
Ericsson OSS
OSS-RC manages Network Elements in the core and access networks for the following domains: GSM, WCDMA, and LTE, which are developed by, or sourced by, Ericsson. OSS-RC supports centralized operation and maintenance of 2G and 3G networks and provides end-to-end management of the radio, core, and backbone networks.
